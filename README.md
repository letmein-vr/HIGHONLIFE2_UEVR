## Features
* **Full 1st Person 6DOF**: Complete 6DOF motion control support.
* **VR Hands**: VR rendered hands with grips for equipped weapons. TBC on adding in finger animations.
* **6DOF Weapons**: Weapons attached to right/left controllers, with VR hands then attached to weapon hand sockets so that weapon animations are intact.
* **Movement**: Movement follows right controller, as does aim / or right-stick seated play.
* **Cinematic Handling**: Automatic camera switching to preserve original cutscenes and in game cutscenes, with VR Hands being hidden and body mesh becoming visible as well as camera changes.
* **Costumes/Suits**: VR hands rebuild with each costume or suit change.

## ⚠️ Known Issues
* **Muppy Doo Fight:** May be able to play this 100% in VR with some difficulties!
* **Right Controller UI Interactions:** Right controller used for UI interactions (i.e. RB/LB), but may be inverted sometimes.
* **Right Controller Movement Direction:** Self explanatory.
* **Dual Wielding Jan and Travis:** Jan is attached to left hand, but aimed with right controller along with Travis.
* **Various graphical issues**
